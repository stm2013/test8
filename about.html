About this website

What kind of website is this? Why?

This website is a part of the course Project Engineering at the Nanoscale held at the faculty of engineering at Lund University. Citing, "The prime aim of the course is to gain knowledge regarding the development of commercial products and processes. The students will learn how to find information in patent databases, and gain knowledge regarding how patents are written and evaluated. The students will use their engineering skills in order to create a prototype of an instrument/device/product related to nanoscience". Here we present our prototype, the proNano, in a marketing fashion.
